Privacy Policy

MindFirst is built on a simple principle: your data belongs to you. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why, and what we never do with it.

1. What we collect

Email address (waitlist)

If you submit your email to join the waitlist, we store it to notify you when features go live. We do not share, sell, or rent your email to any third party. Ever.

Extension data (local only)

The Try First browser extension stores all data — your thinking streaks, session history, and category logs — locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Quiz results

The AI Literacy Score quiz runs entirely in your browser. Your answers and score are not stored on our servers. Nothing is sent anywhere when you complete the test.

Roast tool

Any conversation text you paste into the "Roast My AI Usage" tool is processed entirely in your browser using JavaScript. The text is never uploaded, logged, or stored anywhere outside your device.

Usage log (manual)

The manual usage log page stores data in your browser's localStorage. This data stays on your device and is not synced or shared.

2. What we do not collect

  • No tracking cookies or advertising pixels
  • No browsing history or behaviour analytics
  • No device fingerprinting
  • No sale of data to third parties — ever
  • No AI training on your data

3. Third-party services

MindFirst is hosted on Netlify. Netlify may collect standard server logs (IP addresses, request timestamps) as part of their infrastructure. See Netlify's privacy policy for details.

No advertising networks, analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.), or data brokers are used on this site.

4. Data retention

Waitlist email addresses are retained until you request deletion or we shut down the waitlist. Extension and log data lives in your browser until you clear it manually.

5. Your rights

You can request deletion of your email from our waitlist at any time by emailing us. For extension and log data, you control it directly — clear your browser storage at any time.

6. Children

MindFirst is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

7. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the updated policy.
